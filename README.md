# Hollow Null Requiem

**Dark fantasy roguelike deckbuilder — embrace the corruption or be consumed by it.**

---

## The Hollow Awaits

A cataclysm known as The Sundering tore open the boundary between the living world and the Void, unleashing a tide of corruption across Aethermoor. From the wound in reality grew The Hollow — a shifting labyrinth of fractured spaces where the laws of nature bend and break. Those who enter rarely return, and those who do come back... changed.

But the desperate have no luxury of caution. From the ranks of the fallen, four souls have been resurrected as Requiems — warriors bound to the Void itself, wielding its corruption as both weapon and curse. Each step deeper into The Hollow feeds the darkness within them, eroding their humanity and pushing them toward the Null State: a threshold where overwhelming power comes at the cost of their very existence.

Your task is to lead a team of three Requiems through the branching depths of the Null Rift, building a shared deck of cards, managing the corruption creeping through their souls, and ultimately confronting The Hollow King — the ancient lord who sought to control the corruption and became it.

## The Requiems

### Kira, Ember Blade
**Flame Aspect · Warrior**

Once a soldier of the Hollow Legion, Kira now burns with a fire that consumes everything in its path. Her blade carries the fury of a thousand fallen comrades. She fights with relentless aggression, stacking burn effects that eat through enemies over time. Push her into the Null State and her flames rage out of control — burn effects deal double damage, and every attack ignites.

### Mordren, Shadow Reaper
**Shadow Aspect · Rogue**

A former necromancer who bound his soul to the Void, Mordren walks between life and death. He drains the essence of his enemies to sustain his cursed existence. His cards weaken and debilitate foes while siphoning their strength. In the Null State, his drain effects heal for double, and enemies begin to tear themselves apart — suffering half the damage they deal reflected back upon them.

### Elara, Lightkeeper
**Light Aspect · Cleric**

Once a priestess of the Dawn Temple, Elara channels divine light through the Void. Her healing powers mend both body and soul, though each miracle draws her closer to the darkness. She shields and restores her allies, keeping the team fighting through the deepest reaches of The Hollow. When the Null State takes her, her light turns wrathful — healing effects lash out as damage against enemies, and her radiance pierces straight through enemy defenses.

### Thornwick, Grove Warden
**Nature Aspect · Tank**

An ancient treant spirit bound to the Void, Thornwick remembers when forests covered the world. Now he stands as an immovable guardian, his bark-like skin turning aside all harm. He builds towering walls of block and punishes attackers with thorns. In the Null State, his ancient vitality surges — regenerating health each turn while his defensive abilities grow by half again their strength.

## Key Features

### Team Deckbuilding
Choose three Requiems from the roster and enter the Null Rift with their combined starting deck. Add new cards, upgrade existing ones, and remove dead weight as you progress. Every card choice shapes how your team fights together.

### Corruption & Null State
Corruption accumulates through combat damage, enemy abilities, and the choices you make. As it rises, healing becomes less effective and the danger grows. But at 100 corruption, a Requiem enters the Null State — cards cost less, damage surges by 50%, and their unique Null State ability activates. The trade-off: they lose HP every turn. Knowing when to embrace the edge is the difference between victory and annihilation.

### Procedural Null Rifts
Each run generates a branching map of nodes across three zones. Choose your path through combat encounters, elite fights, Echo Events, shops, sanctuaries, and treasure rooms. No two runs follow the same route.

### Soul Aspects
Five elemental aspects — Flame, Shadow, Nature, Arcane, and Light — form a web of strengths and weaknesses. Flame scorches Nature but falters against Shadow. Shadow devours Light but withers before Nature. Building a team that covers multiple aspects opens strategic options against varied enemies.

### Requiem Arts
As your team fights, they accumulate Soul Essence. Spend it to unleash devastating Requiem Arts — ultimate abilities unique to each character. In the Null State, Arts activate for free.

### Echo Events
Narrative encounters offer meaningful choices with real consequences. Gain powerful advantages, suffer unexpected setbacks, or trigger surprise combat. Every decision ripples through the rest of your run.

### Relics
Passive items discovered in shops and treasure rooms that bend the rules of combat. Boost damage on kill, gain block at the start of each turn, or trigger effects when a Requiem enters the Null State. Build your relic collection to complement your deck strategy.

### Three Zones of The Hollow
- **The Hollow Depths** — Entry to the Null Rift. Weaker enemies test your resolve as you build your deck and learn the corruption's rhythm.
- **The Fractured Halls** — Deeper into the Void. Stronger foes and greater corruption await. Elite encounters grow more frequent.
- **The Null Heart** — The core of corruption. Only the strongest survive to face The Hollow King — a towering skeletal lord in corrupted royal armor, crown fused to skull, who sought to control the corruption and became it.

## How It Plays

Pick your team of three Requiems. Enter the Null Rift. Navigate branching paths, choosing between combat, events, shops, and rest. Fight with cards — deal damage, apply status effects, raise defenses, and manage your team's corruption. Upgrade your deck, collect relics, and push deeper through three zones of escalating danger. Face The Hollow King at the heart of it all.

Designed for mobile with fast runs of 10–15 minutes. Built for Android.
